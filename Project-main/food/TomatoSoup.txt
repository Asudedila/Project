Tomato Soup
1 kg fresh tomatoes,1 onion,2 cloves of garlic,2 tablespoons oil ,1 tablespoon tomato paste
Chop the onion and mince the garlic.
Heat the oil in a large pot. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, saut√© until softened.
Add tomato paste and stir.
If using fresh tomatoes, peel and chop them. If using canned diced tomatoes, add them to the pot.
Add sugar, salt, and black pepper. Stir the ingredients.
Pour in water or vegetable broth and stir.
Once the soup starts boiling, reduce the heat, cover the pot, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes.
Use a blender to puree the soup for a smoother consistency.
Optionally, garnish with fresh mint or parsley before serving.
