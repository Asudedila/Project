Eggplant Casserole with Olive Oil
3 eggplants, sliced lengthwise,1 onion, chopped,3 cloves garlic, chopped,400 grams ground beef ,2 green peppers, chopped,3 tomatoes, grated or chopped,2 tablespoons tomato paste,1/2 cup olive oil,Salt, black pepper,3/2 cup water,2 tablespoons olive oil,1 tablespoon tomato paste
Soak the eggplant slices in salty water, then squeeze out excess water and either fry or bake them until slightly cooked.
In a large pan, heat olive oil. Add chopped onions and sauté until they become translucent.
Add chopped garlic and sauté until fragrant.
Add ground beef or lentils and cook until browned or lentils are cooked.
Stir in chopped green peppers.
Add grated or chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
Pour in 1 cup of water and simmer until it thickens a bit.
Arrange the eggplant slices in a baking dish. Spread the mixture over the eggplants.
In a separate bowl, mix olive oil, tomato paste, and water to make the topping sauce. Pour this sauce over the casserole.
Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius until the top is golden brown.
Optionally, sprinkle shredded cheese on top and bake until melted.
Serve hot or warm.
