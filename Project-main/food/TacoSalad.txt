BEEF,LETTUCE,TOMATO,CHEESE,AVOCADO,ONION
Taco Salad
1 pound ground beef,1 head lettuce,1 cup tomatoes,1 cup cheddar cheese,1 avocado,1/2 onion,Salt and pepper to taste
In a skillet over medium heat, cook the beef. Let it simmer until the meat is thoroughly cooked.
While the meat is cooking, prepare your taco toppings. Shred the lettuce, dice the tomatoes, shred the cheddar cheese,dice the avocado, and finely chop the red onion.
When taco meat is ready, fill each taco shell with a spoonful of the cooked ground beef. Then, add the shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, diced avocado, and chopped red onion on top of the meat.
Add salt and pepper as desired.
