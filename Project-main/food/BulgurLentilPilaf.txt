LENTIL,BULGUR,ONION,OIL
Bulgur Lentil Pilaf
1 cup green lentils, rinsed,1 cup coarse bulgur,1 large onion, chopped,2 tablespoons vegetable oil or butter,2 cups water or chicken broth,Salt,Black pepper,Half a lemon (optional)
Rinse the green lentils and cook them in water until they are tender. Drain any excess water.
In a pot, heat vegetable oil or butter.
Add the chopped onion and sauté until it becomes golden brown.
Add the cooked lentils to the pot and sauté for a few minutes, stirring occasionally.
Add the coarse bulgur to the pot and sauté until the bulgur becomes translucent.
Season with salt and black pepper, and stir well.
Pour in hot water or chicken broth, and stir again.
Cover the pot with a lid, bring it to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer until the liquid is absorbed.
Once the pilaf is cooked, turn off the heat and let it rest with the lid on without stirring.
Optionally, squeeze some lemon juice over the pilaf before serving.
