Vegetable Soup
1 onion,2 cloves of garlic,2 carrots,2 potatoes,1 cup peas (frozen or fresh),2 tablespoons oil,1 tablespoon tomato paste
Dice the onion and garlic. Cut carrots, potatoes, and bell pepper into cubes.
In a large pot, heat vegetable oil. Add diced onion and garlic, sauté until they become golden brown.
Add diced carrots, potatoes, peas. Sauté for a few minutes.
Add tomato paste, stir, and sauté for a couple more minutes.
Pour water or vegetable broth over the vegetables. Bring it to a boil.
Once boiling, reduce the heat, add salt and black pepper to taste, and simmer until the vegetables are tender.
Optionally, you can use a blender to puree the soup for a smoother consistency.
Serve hot.
