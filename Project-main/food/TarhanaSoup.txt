TARHANA,TOMATOPASTE,OIL
Tarhana Soup 
1 cup tarhana ,2 tablespoons oil,1 tablespoon tomato paste
Place the tarhana in a bowl and add some water, stirring to create a mixture. Let it sit for a while.
In a pot, heat the butter or vegetable oil. Add the tomato paste, saut√©ing until well-mixed.
Add the tarhana mixture to the pot and continue stirring and cooking.
Pour in water or chicken broth, stirring to bring it to a boil.
Once it boils, reduce the heat and simmer, stirring occasionally. Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
Season with salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired.
When the tarhana soup reaches the desired consistency, remove it from the heat.
Optionally, garnish with dried mint when serving.
