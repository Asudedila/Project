Cookie
1 cup butter,1 cup white sugar,2 eggs,1 teaspoon baking soda,Â½ teaspoon salt,3 cups flour,2 cups chocolate chips
Beat butter sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.
Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir.
Stir in flour, chocolate chips and salt.
Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
Store in an airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!
