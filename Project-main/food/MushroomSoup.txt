Mushroom Soup
200g fresh mushrooms, sliced,1 onion, chopped,2 cloves of garlic, crushed,2 tablespoons oil,3 tablespoons flour
In a pot, melt butter. Add chopped onion and crushed garlic, saut√© until softened.
Add mushrooms and cook until they release and reabsorb their liquid.
Add flour and stir, cooking for a few more minutes.
Pour in chicken broth or vegetable broth, bring to a boil. Then, reduce the heat and simmer until the mushrooms are fully cooked.
Blend the soup until smooth.
Season with salt and pepper. 
Serve hot.
