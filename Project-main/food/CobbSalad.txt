Cobb Salad
1 head lettuce,2 cooked chicken breasts,4 slices pastrami,2 hard-boiled eggs,1 avocado,1 cup cherry tomatoes,1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese,1/4 cup oil,2 tablespoons vinegar,1 clove garlic,Salt and pepper to taste
In a small bowl, whisk together the oil, vinegar, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined. Set aside.
On a large serving platter or in a bowl, arrange the chopped lettuce as the base.
Arrange rows or sections of diced cooked chicken, crumbled bacon, chopped hard-boiled eggs, diced avocado, halved cherry tomatoes, and crumbled blue cheese over the lettuce.
Just before serving, drizzle the prepared dressing over the Cobb Salad.
