RICE,MILK,SUGAR,VANILLA,CORNSTARCH
Sutlac
2 cups uncooked white rice,4 cups milk,1 cup sugar,1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract,2 tablespoons cornstarch
Bring rice to a boil.
Add milk, sugar and vanilla extract to the rice.
Bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and let it boil very gently for about 10 minutes.
While stirring, gradually pour the milk-cornstarch mixture into the rice.
Remove from the heat and fill small, ovenproof dessert cups, clay cups, or disposable aluminum pudding cups with the hot mixture.
Bake the pudding cups until the tops are nicely browned, about 20 minutes.
Enjoy!
