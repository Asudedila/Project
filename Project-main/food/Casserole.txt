CHICKEN,ONION,GARLIC,PEPPER,CARROT,PEA,POTATO,MILK,OIL
Casserole
4 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless,2 tablespoons oil,1 onion, finely chopped,2 cloves garlic, minced,2 bell pepper, diced,1 carrot, sliced into rounds,1 cup peas (fresh or frozen),2 cups potatoes, diced,1 cup water,1/3 cup milk,Salt and pepper, to taste
Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Brown the chicken on both sides and transfer it to a casserole dish.
In the same skillet, sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until softened.
Add diced red and yellow bell peppers, sliced carrots, peas, and diced potatoes to the skillet. Sauté for a few minutes.
Pour in water and milk. Stir to combine.
Season with salt and pepper. Add flour if you want a thicker sauce. Stir well.
Pour the vegetable mixture over the chicken in the casserole dish.
Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) until the chicken is cooked through, and the vegetables are tender (approximately 30-40 minutes).
Serve hot.
