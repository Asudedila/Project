CHICKEN,MUSHROOM,GARLIC,OIL,PARSLEY,OREGANO
Baked Chicken with Mushroom
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts,450g mushrooms, sliced,1/2 cup (120ml) chicken broth,3 cloves garlic, minced,2 tablespoons olive oil,1 teaspoon dried oregano,Salt and black pepper, to taste,Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
Season the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, dried oregano.
In an oven-safe skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
Add the chicken breasts and sear on each side until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
In the same skillet, add sliced mushrooms and garlic. Sauté until the mushrooms are tender.
Deglaze the pan with white wine (if using) and chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom.
Return the seared chicken breasts to the skillet, nestling them into the mushroom mixture.
Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
Garnish with fresh chopped parsley before serving.
