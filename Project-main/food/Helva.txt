BUTTER,FLOUR,SUGAR,MILK,ALMOND,CINNAMON
Helva
1/2 cup butter,1 cup flour,1 cup sugar,1 milk,2 cups almonds,2 teaspoons cinnamon
In a medium pot over medium heat, melt the butter. Whisk in the flour.
Meanwhile, in a medium pot, combine sugar, water, and milk. Heat them until its boil.
When flour mixture is toasted and browned but not burned, slowly whisk in the warm milk mixture.
Cover pot with a cloth and a lid, then let cool.
Enjoy!
