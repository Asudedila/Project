Greek Salad
2 large tomatoes,1 cucumber,1 onion,1 green pepper,1/2 cup feta cheese,1/4 cup parsley,1/4 cup oil,2 tablespoons lemon juice,1 teaspoon oregano
In a small bowl, whisk together the oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Set the dressing aside.
In a large salad bowl, combine the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, chopped bell pepper, and crumbled feta cheese.
Sprinkle the fresh chopped parsley over the salad ingredients.
Drizzle the prepared dressing over the Greek salad. Gently mix the salad.
