LETTUCE,CROUTON,CHEESE,GARLIC,LEMON,OIL
Caesar Salad
1 head lettuce,1/2 cup croutons,1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese,2 cloves garlic,3 tablespoons oil,2 tablespoons lemon juice,Salt and Pepper to taste
In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, lemon juice, minced garlic,grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper. Whisk everything together until well blended.
In a large salad bowl, add the torn romaine lettuce leaves and croutons.
Pour the prepared Caesar dressing over the lettuce and croutons. Toss gently to coat the lettuce evenly with the dressing.
Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese over the salad and toss lightly again.
You can divide the salad among individual plates and serve it.
