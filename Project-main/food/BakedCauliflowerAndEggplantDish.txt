Baked Cauliflower and Eggplant Dish
1 cauliflower separated into small florets,2 eggplants, sliced lengthwise,1 onion, finely chopped,3 cloves garlic, minced,400 grams ground beef,2 tablespoons tomato paste,1/2 cup olive oil,Salt and pepper, to taste,1 cup water,1 cup shredded cheese (optional),Fresh parsley (for serving)
Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius (350 degrees Fahrenheit).
Boil or steam the cauliflower until it's slightly tender.
Salt the eggplant slices, let them sit, and then pat dry or lightly fry them in a pan until golden.
In a pan, heat olive oil. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
If using ground beef, add it to the pan and cook until browned. If using lentils, add them to the pan.
Stir in tomato paste, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
Add cauliflower and fried or steamed eggplants to the pan. Stir to combine.
Transfer the mixture to a baking dish.
Optionally, sprinkle shredded cheese on top.
Pour 1 cup of water around the edges.
Bake in the preheated oven for about 30-40 minutes or until the top is golden.
Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
