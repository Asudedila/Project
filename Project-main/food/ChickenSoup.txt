CHICKEN,ONION,GARLIC,OIL
Chicken Soup
1 chicken breast (or boiled chicken),1 onion,2 cloves of garlic,2 tablespoons oil
Boil the chicken breast and shred it, or use pre-cooked shredded chicken.
Dice the onion and mince the garlic.
Heat vegetable oil in a large pot. Add diced onion and minced garlic, sauté until they become golden brown.
Add shredded chicken and sauté for a few minutes.
Pour in water and bring it to a boil.
Once boiling, reduce the heat, add salt and black pepper, and continue simmering until the noodles or rice are cooked, approximately 10-15 minutes.
Add finely chopped fresh parsley.
Optionally, add lemon slices when serving for extra flavor.
