BROCCOLI,ONION,RAISIN,ALMOND,VINEGAR
Broccoli Salad
2 heads broccoli,1/2 cup red onion,¾ cup raisins,¾ cup sliced almonds,2 tablespoons vinegar
Wash the broccoli heads thoroughly and cut them into small, bite-sized florets. Place them in a large mixing bowl.
To the bowl with broccoli, add the finely chopped red onion, raisins, and sliced almonds. 
Pour the dressing over the broccoli mixture. Gently toss everything together until the broccoli, onions, raisins, and almonds are evenly coated with the dressing.
