Zucchini Cauliflower Fritters
2 zucchinis,1/2 head cauliflower,1 carrot,1 large onion,2 eggs,1/2 cup all-purpose flour,1/2 cup grated white cheese,1/2 bunch fresh parsley,Salt, pepper,Olive oil (for frying)
Grate zucchinis, cauliflower, and carrot. Finely chop the onion. Chop fresh parsley.
In a bowl, combine grated zucchini, cauliflower, carrot, chopped onion, fresh parsley, eggs, flour, white cheese, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
Heat olive oil in a pan.
Spoon portions of the mixture into the pan and fry until both sides are golden brown.
Place the fritters on paper towels to absorb excess oil.
Serve warm or at room temperature.
