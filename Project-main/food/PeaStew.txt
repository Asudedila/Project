Pea Stew
2 cups peas,1 medium-sized onion, chopped,1 carrot, sliced into rounds,2 tablespoons oil,1 cup water,1 teaspoon sugar,Salt and pepper, to taste,Fresh parsley (for garnish)
Heat vegetable oil or butter in a pot.
Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
Add carrot slices and sauté for a few more minutes.
Add peas and stir.
Pour in water or chicken broth.
Add sugar, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
Cover the pot and simmer over medium heat until the vegetables are tender.
Once the stew is cooked, garnish with fresh parsley.
Serve the pea stew hot.
