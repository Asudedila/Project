LETTUCE,SPINACH,STRAWBERRY,ALMOND,CHEESE,AVOCADO,OIL
Strawberry Salad
6 cups mixed lettuce and spinach,2 cups strawberries,1/2 cup sliced almonds ,1/4 cup feta cheese ,Avocado slices ,1/4 cup oil,Salt and pepper to taste
In a small bowl, whisk together the oil, salt, and pepper until well combined. Set aside.
In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed salad greens with the sliced strawberries, sliced almonds, crumbled feta cheese, and avocado slices.
Drizzle the prepared dressing over the strawberry salad ang gently mix it.
