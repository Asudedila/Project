ZUCCHINI,CARROT,POTATO,LEEK,FLOUR,EGG,OIL
Vegetable Pancakes
1 zucchini,2 carrots,4-5 potatoes,Half a leek (green part),5-6 tablespoons flour,Black pepper,Salt,Red pepper flakes(optional),1 egg,Oil (for frying)
Grate or julienne all the vegetables using the coarse side of a grater or by cutting them into julienne strips.
Add flour, egg, and spices to the vegetables. Mix everything until well combined.
Heat vegetable oil in a wide pan. Use a tablespoon to drop portions of the mixture into the pan.
Fry on low heat until both sides are golden brown, flipping as needed for even frying.
