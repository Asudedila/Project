LENTIL,ONION,TOMATOPASTE,OIL
Mercimek Soup
1 cup lentils,1 onion,2 tablespoons oil,1 tablespoon tomato paste
Rinse the lentils thoroughly.
Finely chop the onion.
In a pot, heat the vegetable oil, add the chopped onion, and sauté until it becomes golden brown.
Add the tomato paste and sauté until its aroma is released.
Add the lentils, stir for a few minutes.
Pour in the water or chicken broth, stir, and bring it to a boil.
Once it boils, reduce the heat, cover the pot, and simmer until the lentils are fully cooked and soft.
Season the soup with salt, black pepper, and optionally red pepper flakes. Blend the soup until smooth using a blender or an immersion blender.
Serve hot, squeezing lemon juice over the soup before serving if desired.
