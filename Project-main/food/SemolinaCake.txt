EGG,SUGAR,OIL,LEMON,VANILLA,FLOUR,SEMOLINA,BAKINGPOWDER
Semolina Cake
3 large eggs,1/2 cup sugar,3/4 cup oil,1 lemon,1 teaspoon vanilla,3 tablespoons flour,1 cup semolina,2 teaspoons baking powder
Firstly, boil the syrup with sugar and water. Cool it down.
Then, add eggs and sugar in a bowl. Whisk them together.
After that, add the other ingredients and mix them all.
Pour the batter into the greased baking pan. Bake until the top is nicely browned.
Using a large spoon, slowly drizzle all of the cooled syrup over the cake and let it soak in.
It is ready to serve. Enjoy!
