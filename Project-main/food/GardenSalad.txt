LETTUCE,SPINACH,TOMATO,CUCUMBER,ONION,PEPPER,LEMON,OLIVE,OIL
Garden Salad
1 small head lettuce,1 small head spinach,2 cups cherry tomatoes,1 cucumber,Â½ onion,1 bell pepper,1 cup olives,Oil and lemon juice (optional) 
Wash and chop the lettuce, spinach  leaves thoroughly. Place them in a large salad bowl.
Wash the cherry tomatoes and cut them in half. Add them to the salad bowl.
Wash and slice the cucumber. Thinly slice the red onion. Chop the bell pepper and add them to the salad bowl.
Add the olives and gently mix all the ingredients together.
Optionally, you can drizzle a dressing made with olive oil and lemon juice.
