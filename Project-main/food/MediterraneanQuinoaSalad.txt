Mediterranean Quinoa Salad
1 cup quinoa,1 3/4 cups water ,1 cup tomatoes,1 cucumber,1/2 cup feta cheese,1/4 cup onion,1/4 cup parsley,Salt and pepper to taste,2 tablespoons lemon juice,1 teaspoon oregano
Combine the rinsed quinoa and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the quinoa is cooked and the water is absorbed. Remove from heat and let it cool.
In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked and cooled quinoa with the halved cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, crumbled feta cheese, finely chopped red onion, and chopped fresh parsley.
Drizzle the prepared dressing over the quinoa salad mixture.
Add salt and pepper if necessary.
