SUGAR,BUTTER,EGG,VANILLA,FLOUR,BAKINGPOWDER,MILK
Cake
1 cup white sugar,½ cup unsalted butter,2 large eggs,2 teaspoons vanilla extract,1 cup flour,1 teaspoons baking powder,½ cup milk
Gather all ingredients.
Cream sugar and butter together in a mixing bowl. 
Add eggs, one at a time, beating briefly after each addition. Mix in vanilla.
Combine flour and baking powder in a separate bowl. Add to the wet ingredients and mix well. 
Add milk and stir until smooth.
Bake in the preheated oven until the top springs back when lightly touched, 30 to 40 minutes.
Remove from the oven and cool completely. Enjoy!
